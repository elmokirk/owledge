# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, technically honest, and specific. Disagreement is normal; low-signal hostility is not.

## Expected Behavior

- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Provide evidence when challenging technical decisions.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the repository.
- Avoid public posting of secrets, customer data, private transcripts, or personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or intimidation
- Doxxing or sharing private data
- Deliberate spam or bad-faith disruption
- Posting undisclosed vulnerabilities in public threads

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, lock, hide, or remove content that violates these rules.
